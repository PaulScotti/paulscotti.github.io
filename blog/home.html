Paul Scotti's Blog

Dr. Paul Scotti is a visiting research scientist at the Princeton Neuroscience Institute working on neuroAI projects together with the Computational Memory Lab (PI: Dr. Ken Norman). Paul was previously Head of NeuroAI at Stability AI and PI of the MedARC Neuroimaging and AI lab. Paul is a strong proponent of "science in the open" and actively leads/mentors various open-source neuroAI projects with an international community of volunteers.
