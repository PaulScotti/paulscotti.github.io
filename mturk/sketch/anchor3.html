Instructions

This experiment will take ~30 minutes to complete. You will be looking at various drawings and then recreating them by adjusting sliders.

At the beginning of each trial you will see a drawing for 3 seconds. Do your best to memorize the drawing while it is shown.

You will then be shown a different drawing, and your task will be to change this new drawing to look like the drawing that was previously shown.

There will be three sliders, each corresponding to a different aspect of the drawing (e.g., eyes, mouth, and chin).

One of the three sliders will be disabled (already set to the correct value).

Do your best to recreate the initially presented drawing on each trial by adjusting the available sliders.

Your progress will not be saved until experiment completion -- do not refresh the browser or you will lose your progress.
